NEED 4 CORN (AAA#pop_42d9Z001, AVF live act, since summer 2022, Berlin)

Need4corn is a multisensory live act that combines noise with taste and smell. As it has been described during its first live performance as "The popcorn makes the music! No you dont understand, the popcorn makes the music!" Need4corn combines the beauty of modulated sound with the necessity of food consumption. The popcorn provides intake and the necessary crowd, noise performances usualy lack, by bringing people together throught their lust for deliciously fresh and salty popcorn.
